The under representation of women in various facets of the society has been a constant point of concern for many. Women not getting equal opportunities in society is a blatant violation of their rights as a human being and so it is important to uncover just to what extent this problem persists in the modern-day world. In order to attain an estimate of how well or poorly the women are placed in the society, as compared to their opposite gender, some key indicators that may be selected to highlight the gender tilt could be looking at how many women are employed in various sectors such as agricultural sector, industrial sector and service sector as opposed to their opposite gender. Another key indicator could be looking at the income gap between the two genders and ultimately, looking at how strongly are women represented in various decision-making bodies across the globe could add some finer detail as well.
This project is an attempt to simply some of these highly concerning statistics and inform the viewers about the seriousness of the situation. In the following tabs to come, we shall discuss the gender tilt in the society by answering the three questions identified which were the key indicators of this imbalance. We shall also be able to interact with the charts and drill down into how each region and country is doing on each of these parameters. Are you sitting down for this?

Conclusion

Looking at all of these plots and the insights, it is pretty evident how the world is off balance when it comes to affording equal opportunities to both the genders. Women are globally not participating nearly as much in employment in the skilled sector as men are, have a higher income gap year on year and are not well represented enough to assert power and make the necessary changes. A lot of initiatives have been taken in this regard such as gender diverse hiring by corporate firms, but it is still a hurdle that we as a species need to leap over and establish a balance in the world.
These plots also highlight how various regions perform on various parameters and allow an insight into the culture and the traditions of the regions which may be leading to such an imbalance in the society. These help us plan more meticulously when trying to tackle such a problem since we gain access to more personalized information about the region and the countries.
